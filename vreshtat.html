Our Esteemed Wineyards

Where tradition meets the earth, crafting wines of exceptional character.

Rahova Valley Vineyard

Nestled in the heart of Rahova Valley, this vineyard is our pride, benefiting from a unique microclimate and mineral-rich soil. It's home to our most revered grape varieties, producing wines with deep complexity and a distinct terroir.

  • Location: Rahova Valley, Albania
  • Elevation: 400m above sea level
  • Soil Type: Clay-limestone, rich in minerals
  • Key Grapes: Vranac, Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot

Bucina Hillside Vineyard

Perched on the sunny slopes of Bucina, this vineyard receives optimal sunlight exposure, perfect for cultivating grapes that require intense warmth. The gentle breezes from the nearby mountains ensure healthy vine growth and concentrated flavors.

  • Location: Bucina Hills, Albania
  • Elevation: 600m above sea level
  • Soil Type: Rocky, well-drained loam
  • Key Grapes: Chardonnay, Sauvignon Blanc, Pinot Grigio

Drini Riverfront Vineyard

Along the serene Drini River, our vineyard benefits from the cooling influence of the water, which moderates temperatures and extends the growing season. This results in grapes with elegant acidity and aromatic complexity, ideal for our refined white wines.
